Asia Pacific University - Malaysia

About APU

Asia Pacific University (APU) is a leading private university in Malaysia, offering a wide range of programs in various disciplines. With a focus on technology and innovation, APU strives to provide quality education and industry-relevant skills to its students.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Business Administration
  • Bachelor of Engineering (Hons) in Electrical & Electronic Engineering
  • Bachelor of Arts in Media Marketing
  • Bachelor of Science in Data Science
  • Master of Business Administration
  • Master of Technology Management

Campus Facilities

  • State-of-the-art classrooms and labs
  • Library with extensive resources
  • Computer and engineering labs
  • Student lounge and recreational areas
  • Sports facilities (gym, swimming pool, etc.)
  • On-campus accommodation
  • Food court and cafes

Location

Asia Pacific University is located in Technology Park Malaysia, Bukit Jalil, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The campus is easily accessible and well-connected to public transportation.
